Alexi And Vakhtang Matchavariani Foundation

Our Mission

1) Popularisation of Georgian Music in Georgia and Abroad.

  • 2) Organisation of professional musical events and their support.
  • 3) Support of young Composers and performers of exceptional talent.
  • 4) Establishment of the International Composers Competition of Alexi Matchavariani.
  • 5) Establishment of the International Youth Symphony Orchestra.
  • 6) Establishment of the musical Festival.
  • 7) Restoration of damaged musical Scores of Georgian Composers.
  • 8) Restoration of Audio & Video samples of old Georgian musical masterpieces.
  • 9) Revival of forgotten Georgian musical masterpieces.
  • 10) Presentation of unknown musical masterpieces in Georgia.
  • 11) Connection of Georgian and foreign young musicians via Music.
